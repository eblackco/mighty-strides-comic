With new LP, Jinx Colton Quartet hits mighty stride

Hi, I'm K.C., and this is my first webcomic!

I've always been interested in storytelling, especially of a visual nature. Those who know me well can attest to me talking about the plotlines and themes of TV shows and movies quite frequently. It also happens that I have a love for all sorts of music, and jazz has always ranked highly in my list of go-to genres. My aim is to explore jazz by combining historical inspiration from some of jazz's pioneers with allegories touching upon modern-day issues, through the admittedly-warped lens of people who just so happen to be cats, dogs and other familiar creatures who are not human.

The idea for Mighty Strides started out as an outline for a short story intended for a writing workshop application. After mulling it around in my head, I decided it'd make a great trial run project to get in the groove of making comics, a hobby I've aspired to do for some time.

I'm so glad you decided to join me on this journey, and I can't wait to see where it leads.
